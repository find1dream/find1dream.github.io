Bellman Equation V(s) Proof

Why we need to understand it?

Bellman equation is a key point  for understanding reinforcement learning, however, I didn’t find any materials that write the proof for it. In this post, I will show you how to prove it easily.

=Eπ[Rt+1+γGt+1∣St=s](1) \begin{aligned} \tag{1} =\mathbb{E}_{\pi}\left[R_{t+1}+\gamma G_{t+1} | S_{t}=s\right] \end{aligned} =Eπ​[Rt+1​+γGt+1​∣St​=s]​(1)

equals to ∑aπ(a∣s)∑s′∑rp(s′,r∣s,a)[r+γEπ[Gt+1∣St+1=s′]](2)\begin{aligned} \sum_{a} \pi(a | s) \sum_{s^{\prime}} \sum_{r} p\left(s^{\prime}, r | s, a\right)\left[r+\gamma \mathbb{E}{\pi}\left[G_{t+1} | S_{t+1}=s^{\prime}\right]\right] \tag{2} \end{aligned} a∑​π(a∣s)s′∑​r∑​p(s′,r∣s,a)[r+γEπ[Gt+1​∣St+1​=s′]]​(2)
